# CustomerDetails Dashboard

## Purpose
Provides a detailed drill-through analysis of individual customer behavior
and payment patterns.

## Key Analyses
- Total Down Payment per Customer
- Number of Purchase Requests per Customer
- Vehicle purchase history

## Visual Components
- KPI Cards for customer-level metrics
- Table displaying detailed transaction history
- Line Chart illustrating customer payment behavior over time

## Interactivity
- Accessed via Drill-through from Customers dashboard
- Context-sensitive filtering based on selected customer

## Business Value
- Identifying high-value customers
- Analyzing repeat purchase behavior
- Supporting customer segmentation and targeting strategies
