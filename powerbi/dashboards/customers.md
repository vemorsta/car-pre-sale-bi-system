# Customers Dashboard

## Purpose
Provides an overview of customer activity and participation in the pre-sale process.

## Key Analyses
- Number of unique customers
- Distribution of purchase requests across customers
- Customer activity by geographic location

## Visual Components
- KPI Card for total customers
- Tables and charts summarizing customer participation
- Aggregated customer activity views

## Filters & Slicers
- Date
- Province
- Car Model

## Interactivity
- Drill-through enabled to the CustomerDetails dashboard

## Business Value
- Understanding customer base size and distribution
- Identifying active customer segments
- Supporting customer-focused analysis
