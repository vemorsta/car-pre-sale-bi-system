# Overview Dashboard

## Purpose
Provides an executive-level overview of the car pre-sale system by summarizing
key financial and operational indicators.

## Key KPIs
- Total Down Payment
- Total Requests
- Total Quantity of Vehicles
- Average Down Payment per Request

## Visual Components
- KPI Cards displaying overall performance
- Donut Chart showing Down Payment share by Car Model
- Line Chart illustrating Down Payment trends over time

## Filters & Slicers
- Car Model
- Dealer Province
- Customer Province
- Date

## Business Value
- Immediate visibility into overall pre-sale performance
- Monitoring cash inflow and demand volume
- Supporting high-level managerial decision-making
