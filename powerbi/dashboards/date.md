# Date Dashboard

## Purpose
Provides time-based analysis of pre-sale performance to identify trends,
patterns, and seasonality.

## Key Analyses
- Monthly and yearly Down Payment trends
- Purchase request volume over time
- Seasonal demand patterns

## Visual Components
- Line Charts for time-series analysis
- Aggregated views by month and year

## Filters & Slicers
- Date hierarchy (Year, Month)
- Car Model
- Dealer

## Business Value
- Supporting demand forecasting
- Identifying peak sales periods
- Improving campaign timing and planning
