# Cars Dashboard

## Purpose
Analyzes demand and financial contribution across car brands and models.

## Key Analyses
- Down Payment distribution by Car Model
- Quantity requested by Car Model and Category
- Average Down Payment per Car

## Visual Components
- Table listing Sum of Down Payments associated with the selected Car informations 
- Clustered Bar Chart for Down Payments comparison with Models
- Clustered Bar Chart for model comparison across regions

## Filters & Slicers
- Car Model
- Category
- Fuel Type
- Date

## Business Value
- Identifying high-demand vehicles
- Supporting product portfolio optimization
- Enabling market share analysis
